Intro

Hey, I'm Arun. I'm a Business & Data Analyst, founder, researcher, and builder of digital infrastructure for things that matter — like health, agri-tech, insurance, and street vendor empowerment.

I'm currently leading backend architecture for a health-insurtech platform integrating with ABHA and NHCX. I'm also sourcing coconuts, building a VR training module for turmeric farmers, and managing pricing APIs for India's rural markets.

Before this, I worked at PayAgri, Agathium, and StartupTN, crafting dashboards, raising funding, and bridging the gap between data and decision-making.

I believe systems thinking can change lives, code can democratize access, and that every farmer deserves a dashboard.
